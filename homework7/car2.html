Nissan Skyline R34

A part of the Skyline GT-R series, this car was introduced in 1999. Though never officialy sold in the U.S., it gained immense popularity.

  • The R34 was popular in motorsports, especially Japanese touring car racing, Super GT, and JDM racing leagues
  • Engine: RB26DETT, a 2.6-liter inline-six twin-turbocharged engine
  • Equipped with a 6-speed manual transmission
