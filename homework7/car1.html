Nissan S13

Known for being drift cars, the Nissan S series first began in Japan in 1988 under the name Silvia, names after the nymph Sylvia who served the Roman goddess of chasity and the hunt.In the North American market it was sold under the name 240SX.

  • Engine options in 1988: KA24E (NA) and CA18DE (Japan)
  • The chassis are well known for being versatile and mod-friendly
  • The s13 was one of the earlier cars to include independent rear suspension (IRS)
